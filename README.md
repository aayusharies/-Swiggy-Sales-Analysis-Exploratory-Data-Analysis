**🍽️ Swiggy Sales Analysis — Exploratory Data Analysis

An end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of Swiggy food delivery transaction data, uncovering revenue trends, geographic demand patterns, restaurant performance, and customer satisfaction signals across 197,430 orders.


📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes 8 months (Jan–Aug 2025) of Swiggy sales data to answer three core business questions:


Where is revenue concentrated — which states, cities, and restaurants drive the bulk of sales?
When do customers order the most — what daily, weekly, and monthly demand patterns exist?
How satisfied are customers — what do ratings reveal about food quality and service?


The analysis combines pandas-based aggregation with matplotlib, seaborn, and plotly visualizations to turn raw transactional data into actionable business insights.
